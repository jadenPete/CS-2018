Introduction

Unlike our previous projects, we developed our ActivityBot together. Similar to the FLiP board, these devices were provided by Parallax to us. We built this bot from the ground up to have wheels, a sensor, a battery pack, and more. The build process took only two days, however getting it working took nearly a week. During this period of trial and error, we encountered issues with everything from the software to the motors, but eventually we produced a functional robot.
